# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

## Introduction

DrinkLion ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the DrinkLion mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information Collection and Use

### Types of Data Collected

**Personal Data:**
We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information without your explicit knowledge. Any personal data you voluntarily provide (such as name, age, health goals) is stored locally on your device only.

**Usage Data:**
We do NOT collect or transmit any usage statistics, analytics, or user behavior data. Our application works entirely offline.

### Data Storage

All user data including:
- User profiles
- Reminder schedules
- Notification preferences
- Reminder history
- Fasting settings
- Quiet hours configuration

...is stored **exclusively on your device** using local SQLite database. We do NOT send this data to any server or cloud service.

### Device Permissions

DrinkLion requests the following permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+) | Send local notifications | Yes |
| RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED | Re-enable reminders after device restart | Yes |
| WAKE_LOCK | Keep device awake when scheduling reminders | Optional |
| SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM | Precise reminder timing | Optional |

**All permissions are used locally only. We do not share any information with third parties.**

## No Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT:
- ✅ Sell your data
- ✅ Share your data with third parties
- ✅ Use your data for targeted advertising
- ✅ Track your behavior
- ✅ Send data to external servers
- ✅ Use cookies or trackers

## Data Retention

Your data is retained solely on your device for as long as you use the application. If you uninstall DrinkLion, all associated data is automatically deleted.

You can manually delete all data by:
1. Going to App Settings → Apps → DrinkLion
2. Selecting "Clear Storage" or "Uninstall"

## Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
